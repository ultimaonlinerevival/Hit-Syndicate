About

Hit Syndicate

Overview

Hit Syndicate is an organized guild built around coordinated PvP and PvM. We use a crime-syndicate theme for identity and structure, but the focus is simple: play together, run events, and keep it fun.

Nobody gets controlled here. Play solo when you want. If we’re raiding or fighting and you’re online, you’re not “required” to show up. The only real expectation is that when we choose to fight together, we don’t scatter.

We run PvP (duels, town/field fights, tournaments, and off-meta ideas) and PvM (treasure maps, champ spawns, raids, and end-game like Doom, Roof, and underwater bosses). We also do custom games and quest-style events built for the guild.

Values

  • No drama
  • Respect guildmates
  • Help each other, especially when it matters
  • Fight together when we choose to fight

Joining

Join through Discord: introduce yourself, run with us once or twice, then we’ll invite you in. Fit and attitude matter more than gear.

What we do

  • Treasure maps, champs, raids, and end-game runs
  • PvP practice at the duel arena (plus real fights when they happen)
  • Template help, suit planning, and gearing up members
  • Skill training and progression support
