About

Hit Syndicate

Overview

Hit Syndicate is an organized Ultima Online guild built around coordinated PvP and PvM. We use a crime-syndicate theme for identity and structure, but the focus is on group play, events, and having fun.

Members are free to play how they want. Solo play is fine, and no one is required to drop what they’re doing to join an event. When we choose to fight or raid together, though, we do it as a group. If we’re outnumbered, we don’t scatter — we stand, learn, and go again.

Our time is split between PvP and PvM. PvP includes duels, town fights, field fights, tournaments, and experimenting beyond the meta. PvM includes treasure maps, champ spawns, raids, and end-game encounters like Doom, the Roof, and underwater bosses. We also run custom events and quest-style activities built specifically for the guild.

Members regularly help each other with skill training, suit building, gear acquisition, and template refinement so everyone can play what they enjoy and feel confident doing it.

Values

  • No drama
  • Respect guildmates
  • Help each other regardless of the situation
  • Fight together when we choose to fight

Joining

Joining starts in Discord. New members introduce themselves, participate in a trial run or event, and receive an invite from there. Activity, attitude, and fit matter more than anything else.
