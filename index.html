Allied Dossier

About

No Quarter Given is an allied collective operating on Atlantic. We span PvP, PvM, events, crafting, and long-term projects across multiple guilds and independent players.

Each allied tag maintains its own identity and leadership while cooperating under a shared banner when interests align.

Principles

  • Respect allied tags and members.
  • Autonomy for each guild.
  • Cooperation over ego.
  • Competence over noise.

Scope

  • PvP (field, group, competitive)
  • PvM (champ spawns, bosses, farming)
  • Events and joint operations
  • Crafting, logistics, and support
  • Media and long-term projects

Affiliation

No Quarter Given is not a single guild. Affiliation may involve joining an allied tag, participating in shared events, or collaborating on specific projects.

Contact leadership or an allied representative for details.
